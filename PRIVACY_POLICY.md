# Privacy Policy for School-Planner (SkoolPlannr / AcademaSync)

**Effective date:** March 3, 2026  
**Project:** School-Planner (SkoolPlannr / AcademaSync)

This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, stored, and shared when you use this project’s applications and services, including the Flutter client, FastAPI/Appwrite backend, and related integrations.

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to:
- The School-Planner apps and APIs in this repository.
- Deployments run by the project maintainers.

If you self-host or redistribute this project, you are responsible for publishing your own privacy policy that reflects your hosting, logs, and data practices.

## 2. Data We Process

Based on the current implementation, the app may process:

### A) Account and identity data
- Email address
- User ID (UID)
- Authentication/session tokens (for sign-in/session handling)

### B) Academic planner data
- Academic year and term details
- Subjects (name, instructor, location, credits, class schedule)
- Tasks (title, description, due date/time, completion state, priority)
- Events (title, type, start/end time, linked subject)
- Grade/assessment entries and computed results (subject scores, SGPA/CGPA related outputs)

### C) Attendance integration data (PESU)
- PESU username and password entered by you
- Optional semester input
- Attendance results returned from PESU (course attendance, totals, percentages)

### D) Basic technical/usage data
- Request metadata and operational logs generated by hosting infrastructure (for reliability, debugging, and abuse prevention)

## 3. Where Data Is Stored

### Cloud/backend storage
Planner/account data is stored in Appwrite collections and services configured for this project.

### On-device storage
The Flutter app stores certain data locally on your device, including:
- Session data in local preferences
- Theme preference in local preferences
- PESU credentials in device secure storage (when you choose to save them)

## 4. How We Use Data

We use data to:
- Create and manage your account and sign-in session
- Sync and display your academic planner data across app screens
- Perform grade calculations and related summaries
- Fetch PESU attendance only when requested by you
- Maintain service security, stability, and troubleshooting

## 5. Attendance Credentials Handling

When you use the PESU attendance feature:
- Credentials are sent to the backend endpoint to perform the attendance fetch.
- Credentials are used to authenticate with PESU and retrieve attendance data.
- Based on current code behavior, credentials are not persisted to the project database by the backend; they may be stored on your device only if you save them in Settings.

## 6. Legal Bases (where applicable)

Depending on jurisdiction, processing is based on one or more of:
- Performance of a contract (providing core app functionality)
- Legitimate interests (security, reliability, debugging)
- Consent (for optional data/actions such as saving PESU credentials)
- Legal obligations (if required by law)

## 7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell personal data.

Data may be processed by third-party service providers needed to operate features, such as:
- **Appwrite** (authentication, database, and function runtime)
- **PESU Academy systems** (only when you request attendance fetch)

Their processing is subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the service, including:
- Until you delete data or your account (where deletion tools are available)
- Longer where required for legal, security, or operational reasons

Local device data remains until you clear app data, sign out, or delete saved values.

## 9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your device and credentials secure.

## 10. Your Choices and Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have rights to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of data
- Object to or restrict certain processing
- Withdraw consent for optional processing

You can also:
- Sign out and clear local session data
- Remove saved PESU credentials from app settings

## 11. Children’s Privacy

This project is intended for students and is not specifically directed to children under the age required by local law to consent to data processing.

## 12. International Processing

If services are hosted in regions different from your location, your data may be processed across borders.

## 13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the project evolves. Updates should include a revised effective date.

## 14. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the project maintainer(s) through the repository issue tracker or your deployment operator.
